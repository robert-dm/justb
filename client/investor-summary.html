justB - Executive Summary

Connecting Tourists with Local Breakfast Providers

Last Updated: January 2026 | Confidential

The Opportunity

justB is a marketplace platform connecting tourists staying in vacation rentals (Airbnb, VRBO, etc.) with local breakfast providers, solving the daily challenge of finding authentic, quality breakfast while traveling.

The Problem

  • 60M+ Airbnb guests annually worldwide lack breakfast options
  • Vacation rentals don't include breakfast service (unlike hotels)
  • Tourists waste vacation time searching for breakfast daily
  • Local breakfast providers struggle to reach tourist customers
  • $85B short-term rental market with no breakfast solution

Our Solution

justB is a mobile-first marketplace platform that enables:

  • For Tourists: Pre-schedule breakfast delivery/pickup for entire vacation stay
  • For Providers: Access to high-value tourist market with predictable orders
  • For Communities: Support local food businesses and authentic experiences

Competitive Advantages

  1. Network Effects: More tourists attract more providers, more providers attract more tourists
  2. Vacation-Specific: Purpose-built for multi-day recurring orders (not ad-hoc delivery)
  3. Pre-Trip Booking: Reduces tourist friction vs. day-of decisions
  4. Local Authenticity: Curated experiences vs. generic chains
  5. First-Mover: No established competitor in this niche
  6. Technology: Proprietary matching algorithm based on location, cuisine preferences, dietary needs

Risk Mitigation

Key Risks & Mitigation Strategies

1. Provider Supply

  • Risk: Difficulty finding quality providers
  • Mitigation: Low barrier to entry, high-margin opportunity, white-glove onboarding

2. Tourist Adoption

  • Risk: Tourists stick with familiar apps
  • Mitigation: Pre-trip marketing, integration with booking platforms, strong value proposition

3. Competition

  • Risk: Uber Eats/DoorDash add vacation features
  • Mitigation: First-mover advantage, curated experiences, vertical specialization

4. Regulatory

  • Risk: Food safety and delivery regulations vary by market
  • Mitigation: Partner with licensed providers, compliance team, insurance coverage

5. Unit Economics

  • Risk: CAC too high or LTV too low
  • Mitigation: Word-of-mouth growth, recurring usage, low churn rate
