Key Assumptions

Growth Assumptions

  • Tourist Conversion Rate: 10% of website visitors book (industry benchmark: 8-12%)
  • Orders per Stay: 3-5 orders per tourist (5-7 day average vacation)
  • Repeat Rate: 40% Year 1 ‚Üí 60% Year 3 (tourists return to vacation rentals)
  • Provider Churn: 30% annually (replaced with new providers)
  • Market Penetration: 5% of tourist market in active cities by Year 3

Revenue Assumptions

  • Commission Rate: 18% average (15-20% range based on provider volume)
  • Average Order Value: $22-26 (increases with trust and upsells)
  • Order Fulfillment: 85% (15% cancellation rate, typical for food delivery)
  • Take Rate Stability: Maintained at 18% (competitive but sustainable)

Cost Assumptions

  • CAC: $25 Year 1, improving to $12 Year 3 (40% via organic/referral)
  • Payment Processing: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction (Stripe standard)
  • Salaries: Market rate for startup talent, scaled with growth
  • Marketing Spend: 30-35% of revenue in growth phase

Conservative Factors

  • Lower market penetration than comparable platforms (5% vs 10-15%)
  • Longer customer payback period (more cautious LTV calculation)
  • Higher CAC assumption (actual may be 20-30% lower with referrals)
  • No subscription revenue included (upside not modeled)
  • No advertising revenue included (upside not modeled)
