The Opportunity

The Problem

60 million Airbnb guests annually face the same frustration: no breakfast service in vacation rentals. Unlike hotels, vacation rentals leave tourists searching for breakfast daily, wasting precious vacation time and missing authentic local food experiences.

Our Solution

justB is a marketplace connecting tourists with local breakfast providers for scheduled delivery or pickup. Tourists pre-book breakfast for their entire stay, while local providers gain access to the lucrative tourist market with predictable, recurring orders.
