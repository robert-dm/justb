# justB - One-Page Investment Teaser

---

## The Problem
60M+ Airbnb guests annually face the same frustration: **no breakfast service in vacation rentals**. Unlike hotels, vacation rentals leave tourists searching for breakfast daily, wasting precious vacation time and missing authentic local food experiences.

---

## Our Solution
**justB** is a marketplace connecting tourists with local breakfast providers for scheduled delivery or pickup. Tourists pre-book breakfast for their entire stay, while local providers gain access to the lucrative tourist market.

---

## Market Opportunity

**$12B Total Addressable Market**
- Global vacation rental market: $113.9B
- Short-term rentals growing 21% annually
- Average tourist: $75-175 per trip on breakfast

**Unique Positioning**
- First-to-market in vacation breakfast niche
- No direct competitors
- Strong network effects
